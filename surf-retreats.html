Surf Retreats

World-class waves. Tropical beauty. All in Costa Rica.

1. Witch's Rock Surf Camp, Tamarindo

Located directly on the beach in Tamarindo, this camp is perfect for all skill levels, from beginners to advanced surfers. They offer unlimited surfboard rentals with access to over 300 boards, daily surf lessons, and guided surf tours to famous breaks like Witch's Rock and Ollie's Point. The camp also provides a beachfront hotel, a daily breakfast at their restaurant, and professional instructors with years of experience.

Learn More

2. Costa Rica Surf Camp, Playa Dominical

This camp in the quintessential surf town of Dominical offers a laid-back, authentic "Pura Vida" vibe. They have been providing surf vacations for over 20 years and treat their guests like family. The camp caters to all skill levels with personalized lessons and a team of local, experienced instructors.

Learn More

3. School of the World, Tamarindo

School of the World offers an immersive "Learning Vacation" where you can combine surfing with other programs like Spanish or Yoga. They have programs for all levels, from beginners to advanced surfers. Advanced surfers can opt for their "Surf Intensive" program, which includes four hours of one-on-one coaching to help them reach the next level.

Learn More

4. Shaka Costa Rica, Santa Teresa

Located in the heart of the Hermosa surf zone, Shaka Costa Rica offers a "genuine surf experience". It's known for its relaxed surf camp vibe, where guests can learn to surf and make lifelong friends. They have been a Traveler's Choice and Certificate of Excellence winner on TripAdvisor since 2013.

Learn More

5. Surf Expedition, South Pacific Coast

This surf camp is located on the south pacific coastline and offers guided surf sessions to 12 different spots in the area. They cater to all levels and even offer a private boat trip to explore remote, pristine waves where you can spot dolphins and whales. The camp also focuses on providing the best food with the best ingredients.

Learn More

6. Blue Osa Yoga Retreat + Spa, Osa Peninsula

This is a place to connect with yourself and the natural world on the Osa Peninsula. The retreat offers farm-to-table cuisine, a chemical-free pool, and a tranquil setting that is perfect for rejuvenating the mind and body. While it's primarily a wellness retreat, its beachfront location makes it a great spot for surf enthusiasts looking for a serene setting.

Learn More

7. Anamaya Resort, Montezuma

Perched in the lush jungle above Montezuma, Anamaya Resort offers a fully customizable experience that caters to surfers of all levels. Professional instructors provide personalized attention to help you reach your goals. They take guests to great surf spots like Playa Grande and Playa Hermosa, which are located near Santa Teresa.

Learn More

8. Bodhi Surf + Yoga, Uvita

Bodhi Surf + Yoga in Uvita has a "Small is Beautiful" philosophy, with a maximum of 10 guests per week to ensure an intimate and personal experience. They focus on creating educational, inspiring, and empowering vacation experiences. This retreat is perfect for those who want to learn to surf and connect deeply with the ocean.

Learn More

9. Tico Loco Surf School, Jacó

Located in the bustling surf town of Jacó, this surf school offers lessons and camps for all levels. Their instructors are known for their patience and expertise, making it a great choice for families and solo travelers alike. The town of Jacó itself offers a vibrant nightlife and plenty of other activities.

Learn More

10. Pura Vida Adventures, Santa Teresa

Pura Vida Adventures has been offering all-inclusive surf camps and yoga retreats since 2004. Located steps from the water at a beachfront resort in Santa Teresa, their retreats include daily surf lessons with a low student-to-teacher ratio, perfectly crafted meals, and daily yoga classes. It’s an ideal spot for finding a balance between adventure and relaxation.

Learn More

11. Safari Surf School, Nosara

This surf school is known for its highly skilled instructors and a comprehensive program that goes beyond just surfing. It's located in Nosara, a town famous for its laid-back atmosphere and consistent waves. They offer a variety of packages for different skill levels, including family-friendly options.

Learn More

12. The Bodhi Tree Yoga Resort, Nosara

While primarily a yoga retreat, The Bodhi Tree is located in a world-renowned surf destination. Surfers can enjoy their incredible amenities and then head out to the waves for a session. It's a great option for a trip where you want to focus on both surfing and wellness, as it offers a blend of adventure and rejuvenation.

Learn More

13. Witches Rock Surf Camp, Tamarindo

Witches Rock is another well-known surf camp in Tamarindo that offers an immersive surf adventure. They provide all-inclusive surf packages and are located directly on the beach. Their programs are designed to cater to all levels, from beginners to advanced surfers looking to push their limits.

Learn More

14. Anamaya Resort, Montezuma

Anamaya Resort is not only for wellness but also offers surfing lessons and packages. It's a fantastic spot for a customizable surf and yoga vacation. The resort's location near world-class surf breaks and stunning ocean views make it an ideal choice for a transformational retreat.

Learn More

15. Surf Expedition, South Pacific Coast

This retreat provides an intimate setting with no more than 12 guests at a time. They take you on boat trips to uncrowded breaks and have an on-site chef who prepares delicious meals to fuel your sessions. It's a unique surf camp experience for those seeking adventure away from the crowds.
