Surf & Wellness Retreats

Ride waves. Restore your mind. All in one retreat.

1. Anamaya Resort, Montezuma

Anamaya Resort offers a fully customizable surf and yoga retreat where you can experience the best of both worlds. Professional instructors provide personalized surf lessons, while the resort’s wellness offerings include yoga with ocean views and bodywork treatments. You can design your own perfect balance of adventure and relaxation.

2. Bodhi Surf + Yoga, Uvita

This surf and yoga camp in Uvita is guided by a "Small is Beautiful" philosophy, ensuring an intimate and personal experience for a maximum of 10 guests per week. The retreats are designed to be educational and empowering, helping you to improve your surfing skills while also connecting with the ocean through yoga.

Join the Journey

3. Pura Vida Adventures, Santa Teresa

Located in Santa Teresa, Pura Vida Adventures combines all-inclusive surf camps with yoga retreats for a perfect balance of adventure and restoration. They offer daily surf lessons from expert instructors and daily yoga classes in a beachfront, open-air studio.

See the Schedule

4. Rise Up Surf Retreats, Osa Peninsula

Perched on the edge of a virgin rainforest with direct access to perfect waves, this retreat is an idyllic blend of luxury and eco-appeal. It’s ideal for beginners and intermediate surfers, offering surf coaching, video analysis, and daily yoga sessions, as well as adventures like waterfall excursions and horseback riding.

Join the Journey

5. Surf Expedition, South Pacific Coast

This retreat offers an integrated experience with daily surf sessions and "yoga for surf" classes on an outdoor platform. They take you on boat trips to uncrowded breaks and offer private surf coaching and video analysis. The environment, with no Wi-Fi and plenty of hammocks, is designed for you to take a step back and relax.

See the Schedule

6. Blue Osa Yoga Retreat + Spa, Osa Peninsula

Blue Osa is a great option for an integrated retreat, combining yoga and wellness with the opportunity to surf. Located on a remote part of the Osa Peninsula, it offers a tranquil environment for your yoga practice, with the waves just steps away for a surfing session. It's a true sanctuary for the mind, body, and soul.

7. The Bodhi Tree Yoga Resort, Nosara

The Bodhi Tree is a renowned yoga resort located in Nosara, a famous surf destination. It’s an ideal spot for those who want to focus on their yoga practice while having the option to surf. The resort offers beautiful accommodations, an infinity pool, and healthy organic meals to fuel both your yoga and surf sessions.

8. Witch's Rock Surf Camp, Tamarindo

Witch's Rock offers all-inclusive surf packages, but also has a focus on wellness. Their beachfront location and positive vibe make it easy to relax and recharge. After a day of surfing, you can unwind and enjoy the tranquility of their beachfront hotel.

9. Shaka Costa Rica, Santa Teresa

Shaka Costa Rica combines a genuine surf experience with a relaxed atmosphere in Santa Teresa. While primarily a surf camp, they offer a laid-back vibe that promotes relaxation and connection. It’s a great option for a trip focused on both physical activity and rest.

10. School of the World, Tamarindo

School of the World offers a unique "Learning Vacation" where you can combine surf and yoga for a perfect balance of body and mind. The surf schedule is designed to give you time between sessions to take other classes, such as yoga, which is a great way to stretch and recover.

11. Samadi Retreats, Santa Teresa

Samadi Retreats is known for its customized yoga and surf retreats in Santa Teresa. They work with local surf instructors and yoga teachers to create a personalized experience. It's an excellent choice for a small group or private retreat, offering an intimate and tailored journey.

12. Bodhi Tree Yoga Resort, Nosara

As a world-renowned yoga resort, Bodhi Tree offers an exceptional wellness experience. Its location in Nosara, a surf mecca, makes it a perfect place for those who want to practice yoga and also get out in the waves. It's a sanctuary that supports both an active and restorative lifestyle.

13. Tico Loco Surf School, Jacó

Located in the lively town of Jacó, this surf school offers a fun and active experience. You can combine your surf lessons with other wellness activities in the area, such as yoga studios and spa services. It's a great option for a more flexible and self-directed retreat.

14. AmaTierra, Turrubares

AmaTierra is a unique retreat that blends herbal medicine with wellness. While not directly on the coast for surfing, it's a great choice for those who want to get away from the beach and focus on holistic health. You can still arrange surf trips from this location, making it a flexible option for a surf and wellness combo.

15. Montezuma Yoga, Montezuma

Located in a vibrant beach town, Montezuma Yoga provides daily classes and a focus on self-discovery. You can combine your daily yoga with surfing lessons from local schools, creating a personalized retreat that balances activity and relaxation in a beautiful, natural setting.
