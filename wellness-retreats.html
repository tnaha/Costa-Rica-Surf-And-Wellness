Wellness Retreats

Find stillness, strength, and serenity in Costa Rica.

1. Anamaya Resort, Montezuma

Anamaya Resort is a premier destination for yoga retreats and transformational wellness, nestled in the lush jungle above Montezuma. They offer a variety of experiences, from customizable retreats to specialized programs led by world-class facilitators. The resort also provides organic meals, a salt water infinity pool, and spa treatments.

Explore This Retreat

2. Bodhi Tree Yoga Resort, Nosara

Bodhi Tree Yoga Resort is a luxurious boutique hotel in Nosara that was designed to flow with nature. It features an open-air yoga studio, an infinity pool, and healthy meals made with fresh ingredients. They are also a plastic-free facility and use solar panels for hot water.

Explore This Retreat

3. Tabacón Thermal Resort & Spa, Arenal

Located at the foot of the majestic Arenal Volcano, Tabacón is the only resort in the area with a naturally flowing thermal river. They are pioneers in thermal tourism and offer a unique wellness concept. Guests can enjoy the mineral-rich thermal springs, an award-winning spa, and guided nature tours.

Book Your Stay

4. Pura Vida Retreat & Spa, Costa Rica's Central Valley

Pura Vida Retreat & Spa is a rejuvenating retreat center located in the picturesque coffee plant-draped hills of Costa Rica's Central Valley. The mountaintop haven offers invigorating yoga, specialized wellness therapies, and wholesome cuisine. It is recognized as one of the most experienced retreat centers worldwide.

Book Your Stay

5. Tierra Encantada, Nosara

This retreat is designed to help you reconnect with your true self through a blend of yoga, mindfulness, and holistic healing. Located in the lush jungles of Nosara, the "Mind Body Soul" retreat includes a variety of wellness treatments like cold plunges, an infrared sauna, and a neuroacoustic table session.

Explore This Retreat

6. Imiloa Institute, Dominical

Imiloa Institute is a 5-star retreat center in Dominical that hosts some of the world's leading educators and entrepreneurs. The campus offers full nature immersion and gourmet food, and everything is included, from luxury transportation to a large staff that looks after your every need.

Explore This Retreat

7. The Point Luxury Villa, Guanacaste

This luxury wellness retreat offers a unique blend of opulent decor and nourishing self-care. Guests can enjoy personalized yoga classes, guided meditation sessions, and spa and massage services led by professional therapists. The villa provides stunning views of the Pacific Ocean and easy access to two beaches.

Learn More

8. Peace Out Yoga, Nosara

This retreat is held at the Bodhi Tree Yoga Resort, a world-renowned retreat center in the jungle along the ocean. It is a seven-night stay that includes accommodations, three gourmet and organic meals a day, and daily meditation, breathwork, and yoga sessions.

Learn More

9. Villa Punto de Vista, Manuel Antonio

Located above the rainforest and coastline of Manuel Antonio, this luxury villa is the perfect location for a tranquil wellness retreat. It offers a large, open space for holding yoga and meditation sessions and is surrounded by rainforests, waterfalls, and exotic wildlife. Guests can also hire a private chef for their stay.

Learn More

10. Kinkára, Southern Pacific Zone

An eco-luxury retreat experience in the Southern Pacific Zone. Kinkára offers an immersive experience in nature with permaculture gardens, sustainable practices, and unique accommodations. It’s an excellent choice for a retreat focused on eco-consciousness and disconnecting from the modern world.

Learn More

11. Rythmia Life Advancement Center, Guanacaste

Rythmia is a wellness center known for its plant-based medicine ceremonies, yoga classes, and farm-to-table organic food. It offers a structured program for self-discovery and transformation, with a strong focus on spiritual growth and healing. It is a highly-rated option for those looking for a deep, transformative experience.

Learn More

12. The Harmony Hotel, Nosara

The Harmony Hotel is a wellness-oriented hotel that is half yoga retreat and half beach escape. It offers daily yoga classes, a spa, and healthy cuisine. It's an ideal choice for a retreat that balances structured wellness with a laid-back, leisurely vacation.

Learn More

13. Blue Osa Yoga Retreat + Spa, Osa Peninsula

Located on the Osa Peninsula, Blue Osa is an all-inclusive eco-resort that offers a sanctuary for the mind, body, and soul. They have a variety of yoga retreats, a farm-to-table culinary experience, and a spa. It's an immersive experience for those looking for deep relaxation in a pristine natural environment.

Learn More

14. AmaTierra, Turrubares

AmaTierra is a tranquil retreat center and eco-lodge that combines traditional herbal medicine with holistic wellness. It is located in the beautiful mountains of Turrubares and offers yoga, meditation, and natural therapies. It's a great option for those seeking a more traditional and herbal-focused healing experience.

Learn More

15. Montezuma Yoga, Montezuma

Located in the heart of Montezuma, this retreat is known for its daily yoga classes and a focus on self-discovery. It offers a variety of accommodations and packages that allow you to customize your experience to include local excursions and healing therapies.
